How does it work?

Getting started is easy! With Wanderlust Videos, you’re just a few clicks away from offloading weeks of editing work and get top quality post-production services. Get started today and see how easy it really is.
Request a call Start a project
1
Send us your footage
Share your raw footage, notes, and any style references through our platform. If uploading isn’t ideal, you can mail us a hard drive. We’ll handle transferring the files to our cloud!
2
We edit & manage feedback
Our editing team gets to work while your dedicated project manager keeps everything on track. We handle internal feedback and revisions so you don’t have to go back and forth.
3
Get your final video
Once the edit passes our internal review, we’ll send it your way. Need tweaks? We're always happy to make adjustments. All final files are delivered in your preferred format.
